# 🫀🧠 ANATOMIA
### AI-Powered Interactive Medical Digital Twin

> **"See Beyond Anatomy. Experience Life Within."**

ANATOMIA is an AI-powered immersive medical education platform that transforms the way users learn human anatomy. Instead of reading static textbooks or viewing images, users explore realistic 3D organs, travel through blood vessels and neural pathways, visualize disease progression, interact with AI tutors, and experience anatomy in AR/VR.
